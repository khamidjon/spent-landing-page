# **2.1.1**
The current release of Spent — log a spend in 3 seconds:

- Snap a receipt and Spent's AI fills in the merchant, amount, date, category and line items
- Say a spend out loud ("Coffee, 45 thousand") and Spent parses it into a clean transaction
- Fast manual add/edit with categories, tags, dates and recurring rules
- One-screen home: a hero total, category chart and day-grouped transactions
- Insights: a category donut with period-over-period trends, plus per-category budgets with gentle nudges
- Multi-currency with automatic conversion and precise decimal money
- Multiple lists — Personal, Family and Business — with sharing
- Export any period to CSV or PDF
- Local-first and fully offline, with private cloud sync; start anonymously or sign in with Apple or Google
- Full light and dark themes, plus optional reminders, weekly summaries and big-spend alerts
